Harvard's CS50 Certification (In Progress)

To prepare myself for university, I took on the challenge of completing Harvard's first-year computer science course, better known as CS50. The course began on familiar territory, starting with the fundamentals of computer science and syntax in the C programming language. Afterwards, we were introduced to Python and some web development using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. It was also using this information that I was able to design and maintain this website. The course content is brilliantly compiled and easy to follow due to the exceptional teaching style of the professor, David Malan.

For me, the most challenging aspect of this course were the assignments that accompany each lecture. But this is what I appreciate about this course, since it challenges me to use my creative thinking abilities in a way I have not been forced to before. As of today, I have completed more than half the course and will keep updating this section everytime progress is made.

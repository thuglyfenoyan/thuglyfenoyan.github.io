Varsity Boy's Table Tennis Team

During Grade 11 and Grade 12, I was part of my school's Varsity Table-Tennis team. Starting off as an unexperienced singles, I slowly improved various aspects of my game and transformed into a doubles player. I was part of 2 main competitions during both seasons; the regular season and ROPSSAA championships. The regular season was a round-robin style tournament where schools competed against each other in a best-of-7 match. The ROPSSAA championships was a knockout tournament designed to crown the best players in each category.

St Joseph's Varsity Boys' Table Tennis Team, 2022/23

In Grade 11, the regular season was quite disappointing according to my standards. Our school was only successful in getting 2 victories throughout the season. However, the ROPSSAA champtionships was a different story. I placed 6th with my friend for Senior Boys' Doubles in my division. It was a great finish to my debut season and a wonderful start to my final year.

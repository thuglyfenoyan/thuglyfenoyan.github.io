FIRST Robotics Team Programming Sublead

Following a succession of programming course completions and certificates in Grade 10, I joined my school's robotics team, FRC 4015. The team consisted of 4 subteams, namely mechanical, electrical, design and programming. In grade 11, I was promoted to sub-lead for the programming department. My job was to program the main subsystems of the robot and to nurture junior students in the world of programming.

St Joseph's Robotics Team Yearbook Picture, 2022/2023

Our team was never a formidable force; we only participated in district events with no success. However, that changed in 2022 when we won the Humber College District Event. For the first time in school history, our team had won a gold medal, ultimately securing us a spot in the 2022 FRC Ontario Provincial Championships (also the first time in school history).

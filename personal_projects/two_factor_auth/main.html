Arduino Two Factor Authentication Door Lock

A continuation of my previous project, the two factor authentication door lock system implemented both the RFID technology and a membrane keypad to ensure maximum security. In order to unlock the door, one must possess the correct card and enter the 5-digit passcode. The RFID in this project was programmed through an Arduino Nano and the rest of components were controlled through an Arduino Mega.

Project Demonstration

This project taught me a lot. I learnt the importance of a good design and organized program, since I spent most of the time online finding solutions for the problems I encountered. The lessons I learnt through the building of this prototype is still applied in all my projects today.
